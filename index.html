Nostalgia and its Importance in Marketing

This website gives you an overview of how nostalgia impacts our perception of the worth of an idea, with a particular interest in marketing of products. The Saregama Carvaan provides a case study to look at how nostalgia can be directly used to bring people to their past.

Nostalgia acts in other fields of life.

It impacts us in ways we might never think of if we aren't paying attention to how we're making decisions.

Rosy Retrospection plays a big role in us percieving the good old days as the good old days, hence making us feel that the past is something to be chersished and the present something to suffer through.

We need to be aware of how nostalgia plays a role in our decision making so that when we make a choice, we make it knowing that it might be nostalgia that is making us lean towards a particular option.

Many sources have been used to compile this website with accurate data and terminology.
